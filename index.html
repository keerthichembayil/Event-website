Who are we?
Lets connect together for the best

Altruistic soceity community is a group of people in the local area of villangad taluk Kochi,Kerala.Community is conducting events since 5 years for the development of social interaction among community.We conduct events related to various fields.The profit we earned after conducting events is transfered to the charity fund which is used for the community welfare and charity.
